About

John Lundun

John Lundun is a SAMA-Nominated artist and SAMA-winning music producer and composer, specializing in creating iconic sound for major brands, television, and film. With a professional foundation from a Damelin College certificate in Contemporary Music and a TUT Diploma in Jazz & Popular Music, Lundun expertly bridges artistic innovation and commercial excellence.

Industry Recognition & Academic Foundation

SAMA-Nominated artist and SAMA-winning music producer and composer with a professional foundation from a Damelin College certificate in Contemporary Music and a TUT Diploma in Jazz & Popular Music — bridging artistic innovation and commercial excellence.

Music Composition & Final Mix Engineering for Brands & TV

Lundun Audio is the creative force behind memorable and effective TV themes and sonic branding for premier clients including Nando’s, Game Stores, Tropika, Sony Music, the JSE and MSM Property Fund. His expertise ensures that every project is not just heard, but felt — driving engagement and brand identity through impeccable composition and sound design.

A Trusted Collaborator to South Africa’s Music Elite

With a versatile, genre-spanning expertise, Lundun has produced, composed, and engineered alongside an illustrious roster of top South African artists. His collaborative credits include: K.O, Kabza De Small, Kwesta, Jesse Clegg, Manana, Samthing Soweto, Msaki, Berita, Ndlovu Youth Choir, Sun El Musician, Thando Zide, Simphiwe Dana, and many more.

Innovator in Sound: From Lo-Fiano to Film Scores

Pioneering the new genre Lo-Fiano (a fusion of Amapiano and Lofi) in collaboration with Apple Music, Lundun is at the forefront of musical trends. His scoring credentials for film and TV such as Outlaws, Piano Love on Netflix, Mpondoland, and Mkhize to Masemola highlight his ability to craft compelling narratives and emotional depth through music.
